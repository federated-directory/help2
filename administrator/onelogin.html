Integrate with OneLogin

In case your company uses OneLogin as their identity and access management solution, a lot of contact data is already centrally available. Which is great, because you can provision your OneLogin users directly to your Federated Directory.

Users within the directory of OneLogin can also login to Federated Directory with their OneLogin account.

We are finalizing this document at the moment. Until then: if you need help with this integration, let us know.
